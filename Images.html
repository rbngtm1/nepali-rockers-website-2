Career

Prateek Singh Thapa started singing since his childhood. He started his music career by establishing a band name "Albinos Wallaby". This band had struggled about 3 to 4 years to play in the good pubs of Kathmandu. The dream finally came true as the band performed many great performances in most famous pubs of Nepal like Trisara and Victory Lounge.
